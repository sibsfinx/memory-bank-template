# Product Vision

This document records the stable direction of the product. It should help make decisions between competing features, but does not replace PRD, roadmap, or domain rules.

## Product Promise

Describe in 1–3 paragraphs what outcome the product promises the user or customer segment.

## Strategic Bets

| Bet ID | Bet | Why now | Evidence | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `BET-01` | What we consider an important bet | Why this is relevant now | What it is based on | When we review |

## Experience Principles

- `XP-01` A principle that must be preserved across all key product surfaces.
- `XP-02` A trade-off the product makes intentionally.

## Product Non-Goals

- `PNG-01` What the product consciously does not try to solve.
- `PNG-02` Which audience, use case, or business model we are not optimizing for right now.

## Decision Rules

Describe rules that help choose between two initiatives.

Example:

- If two initiatives have comparable impact, priority goes to the one that improves the core workflow from [`context.md`](context.md).
- If an initiative requires a new domain concept, first update [`../domain/model.md`](../domain/model.md) and [`../domain/rules.md`](../domain/rules.md).
